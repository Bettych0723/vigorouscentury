FAQ

What are the service items of Vigorous Century Corporation Limited?

The service items of Vigorous Century Corporation Limited include: 1. 3D art design. (mainly game art) 2. Animation design. 3. Game soundtrack design. 4. Visual design

Can you provide quotation information for art outsourcing?

Since the style, specification, quantity, quality, and manufacturing process of art production output are very different, for example, the price difference between a small number of special production models and relatively large but common production models may not be large, so it is impossible to list them all. Therefore, the price needs to be judged based on actual demand interviews and the list of style, specification, quantity, quality, and manufacturing process provided by the customer, so that the quotation can be more accurate.

How long does it take to make a animation film?

After the script is confirmed, the production time is about 1 to 2 months, depending on the length and complexity of the video production, or it will be planned according to the project schedule, depending on the client's video production needs and discussions.

How will the copyright of the game soundtrack be handled?

You can choose to keep the copyright in our company, or buy out the copyright.

I have an idea for cooperation, how should I contact Vigorous Century Corporation Limited?

Welcome to write directly to vigorouscentury@gmail.com for inquiries, or fill out the form to us, and we will have professional staff to serve you.
